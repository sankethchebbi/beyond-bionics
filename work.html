EEG - (Electroencephalography) sensors are used to measure the electrical activity in the brain. EEG sensors are often used in research, medicine, and other fields to help understand how the brain works and to diagnose conditions such as epilepsy.While there are certainly more advanced EEG sensors available, the FT&S MindLink EEG headband is popular sensor due to its ease of use and affordability. It uses dry electrodes, making it less messy and more convenient than wet electrode systems. The headband is also lightweight and comfortable.

Cys-S8218 Servo Motor - A servo motor is a crucial component in robotics and automation systems, designed to provide precise control over angular position, velocity, and acceleration. Typically, servos operate based on feedback control systems, using a control signal to command the motor to move to a specific position. The servo offers a stall torque of 38kg.cm at 6.0V, making it suitable for tasks requiring substantial force. Its control system utilizes Pulse Width Control, with a neutral point at 1500Âµsec. With its specific electrical characteristics and robust design, the CYS-S8218 servo motor is an integral component for applications demanding precise and powerful motion control.

Arduino Uno - The Arduino Uno is a versatile microcontroller board widely used in electronics projects and prototyping, features digital and analog input/output pins, making it suitable for various applications. The Arduino Uno operates on a 5V supply voltage and is programmed using the Arduino IDE, a user-friendly development environment. In our project, we plan to integrate the Arduino Uno to control the CYS-S8218 servo motor. Using the digital output pins, we send pulse-width modulation (PWM) signals to the servo, specifying the desired position. The servo's control wire will be connected to one of the digital pins, allowing the Arduino to precisely manipulate the motor's angular position.

HC-05 Bluetooth module widely employed for wireless communication in electronic projects. With its serial communication capabilities, the HC-05 facilitates the establishment of Bluetooth connections between devices. In our project, we aim to integrate the HC-05 module to establish a wireless link between an EEG (electroencephalogram) sensor and our central processing unit, an Arduino . The EEG sensor will transmit attention-related data to the HC-05 module, which will then relay this information to the microcontroller. Our programming logic on the microcontroller will interpret the received data and potentially trigger actions or display information based on the user's attention levels .This wireless connection enhances the flexibility and mobility of our EEG-based project, allowing users to interact with the system without being tethered to physical connections.

Condition Checking - The code first checks if the debugging output is disabled (!DEBUGOUTPUT) and if a significant data packet (bigPacket) is received.
LED Control: If the received data packet has good quality (poorQuality == 0), the LED is set to HIGH; otherwise, it is set to LOW. This serves as a visual indicator of the signal quality.
Attention Monitoring: The code prints the "Attention" value to the serial monitor, indicating the user's attention level based on EEG readings.
Servo Activation: If the attention level exceeds 60 and the servo is not already activated (!servoActivated), the servo motor is attached to pin 9, activated, and instructed to move. The servo is then deactivated after a delay, mimicking a gripping motion.
Servo Deactivation: If the servo was previously activated (servoActivated), it is detached to conserve resources and prepare for potential future activations.
